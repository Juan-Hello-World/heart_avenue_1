Work Reports & Activities

Read here about all our work details

Education & creativity

We focus on reading routes, literacy programs, and story readings, encouraging children to create their own stories. We also promote art dissemination, organize medical activities, and run essential donation campaigns. These programs are designed to provide a nurturing environment where learning thrives and creativity is celebrated.

Global reach

Our programs have a far-reaching impact, operating in a hybrid model that combines international virtual initiatives with in-person programs across more than 10 countries. Physically, we have established branches in Bahrain, South Africa, Florida, the United States, Peru, and Colombia, serving nine regions worldwide.

Romanticism & art

We have a deep commitment to artistic expression and creative work, especially with children. Our workshops and projects are crafted to be visually engaging and to create a safe space where children feel comfortable expressing their tastes, concerns, and interests authentically. 

"The real world sometimes shatters the glass ahead of time. We embrace an artistic and romantic identity to help children process their environment and prevent violence through metaphor, emotional release, and psychological processing."
